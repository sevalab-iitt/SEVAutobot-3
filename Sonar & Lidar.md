#### Both Sonar and LiDAR are remote sensing technologies used to detect objects and map environments by emitting signals and measuring how they bounce back.
### Sonar (Sound Navigation and Ranging)
Sonar uses sound waves to "see" in environments where light cannot travel effectively
### LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging)
LiDAR uses laser light pulses (typically near-infrared) to create high-resolution.
